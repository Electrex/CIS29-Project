SPACE INVADERS

The goal of this game is to destroy all of the space invaders before they
destroy you.  Use the left and right arrow keys to move your laser gun.
Press the space bar to shoot the gun.  You can be killed by a bomb or if
the invaders reach the bottom of the screen and squash you.  


SCORING

Shoot the laser gun      -1
 
Top row invader           5

Second row invader        4

Third row invader         3

Fourth row invader        2

Bottom row invader        1

The red flying saucer    10

A dropping bomb          20
